System Administration Configuration – Settings

In addition to configuring core data items that are specific to your organization, you must configure settings that determine a variety of operational factors for your EMS installation, such as system-wide parameters, registration information, email settings, and Reservation Wizard required fields.

This chapter covers the following topics:

  • Editing System Parameters
  • Importing Registration Information
  • Configuring the Confirmation Email Subject Line
  • Configuring Email Settings
  • Configuring Required Fields in the Reservation Wizard
