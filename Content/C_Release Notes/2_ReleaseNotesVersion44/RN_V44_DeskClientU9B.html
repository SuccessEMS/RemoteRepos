Floor Plan Utility
Selected Floor Not Saved When Placing Multiple Floor Map Indicators
When users selected a floor and placed a floor map indicator, then saved, and started to place another indicator, the floor selection was not saved. 

Fix: Modified validations to ensure that the last floor the user chose is saved such that when dragging or clicking to map another location, the user does not have to re-select the floor.
