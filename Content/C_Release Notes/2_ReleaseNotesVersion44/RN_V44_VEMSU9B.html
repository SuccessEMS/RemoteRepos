Submit Button Missing For Service-Only Requests

When users requested a service-only meeting with an override description room and at least one associated service, once they had completed the "When and Where" section and clicked the Get Services button, the Submit button ceased to display.

Fix: Modified functionality so that the Submit button displays as expected during the booking process regardless of service requests.
