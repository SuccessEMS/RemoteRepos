Geolocation: Mapping, QR Codes, and Floorplans

You can associate rooms with QR Codes: {{product_dtc}} provides a report that generates the codes and associates them with the designated rooms. You can print room cards that attendees can then scan or swipe (room signs or using mobile device)s to check-in to meetings; {{product_mobile}} integrates with mobile devices' geographic mapping functionality to enable users to locate nearby rooms and to book space and check into meetings. Building floorplans and room layout images can be configured to visually show user location, room proximity and location on a campus and on a floor, and room drawings. See Also:  QR Code Configuration Overview, Floorplan Configuration Overview, and EMS Floor Plan Installation Overview.
