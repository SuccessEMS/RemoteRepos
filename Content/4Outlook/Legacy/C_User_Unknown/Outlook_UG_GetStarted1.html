Getting Started With EMS for Outlook

{{product_emso}} is an optional add-on for Microsoft® Outlook; if you have it installed, you will see the {{product_emso}} icon in the top toolbar of your Outlook application window. This tool enables you to easily use Outlook to search for available rooms throughout your EMS database and make a reservation without exiting the application. Once you begin a meeting in Microsoft® Outlook, you can access the add-in by clicking the EMS icon.
