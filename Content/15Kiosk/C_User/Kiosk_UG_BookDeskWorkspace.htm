Booking a Desk or Workspace in EMS Kiosk App

The Make a Reservation page in EMS Kiosk App allows you to make your own reservations for today.

Note: The following procedure is written from the perspective of a group search; however, depending on how your EMS Kiosk App is configured, you might be required to enter a personnel number/employee ID number for your search criteria.

  1. Click Make a Reservation. A page opens where you find your user profile in the system (unless your system is set up to allow guest reservations).

Group/Person Search Page

  1. In the Group field (which may be labeled anything your Administrator prefers, such as "Employee"), enter your name, and then click Search. A list of all groups/people that meet your search criteria is displayed. The search is limited to the exact order of the characters in the string but the string can be found anywhere in the search results and it is not case-sensitive. For example, a search string of “ed” returns both Anderson, Scott and Evans, Dean.

Example of Search Results for a Group/Person

  1. Select your name and then press Continue.

Continue Screen

  1. The Make a Reservation page opens. 

Note: Based on how your organization has configured EMS Kiosk, various fields might or might not be available.

Make a Reservation Default Page

  1. Modify the values for dates and times as needed.
  2. If floorplans have been configured for any rooms in the Kiosk profile, then the floorplan view will show by default. You can view a list view of the available spaces by clicking the ‘Switch to List View’ button at the bottom.
  3. If your Admin has enabled filtering, you can restrict results by selecting a filter (e.g., you can filter by room type and floor). 
  4. To reserve from the Map View, select an available room and click the Reserve button.
  5. To reserve space from the list, click the Reserve icon next to the space that you want to reserve for your event.
