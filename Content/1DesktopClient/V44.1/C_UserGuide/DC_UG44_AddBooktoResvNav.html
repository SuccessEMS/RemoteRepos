Adding Bookings to a Reservation in the Navigator

You can use the Navigator to add one or more bookings to an existing reservation. See Also: Reservation Structure.

  1. Open the reservation in the Navigator. 
  2. In the left pane of the Navigator, select the reservation to which you are adding the bookings, and then in the Bookings pane, click New. The Reservation Wizard opens. See Making a Reservation Using the Reservation Wizard.
