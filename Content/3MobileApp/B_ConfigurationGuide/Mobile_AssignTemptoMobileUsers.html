Assigning Templates to EMS Mobile Users

V44.1 allows you to select which process templates (e.g "web process templates") will be enabled on your users' mobile devices. From the Admin page for templates in the , you will see an Enable for Mobile checkbox on the first tab of the template dialog box: 

 

Note: is designed to make and edit simple reservations for users "on the go." At this time it cannot handle service requests, video conference bookings or complex workflows.  Please consider this when you decide which templates should be enabled for the . Additionally, you can only change the name and icon of the mobile app through private deployment via MDM. Please refer to your MDM guide for instructions on how to change the name and icon of the mobile app.
