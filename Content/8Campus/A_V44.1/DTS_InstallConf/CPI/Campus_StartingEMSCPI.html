Starting the

Verify your installation by opening a browser and entering the address:

http://[ServerName]/CampusPlanningInterface/  (replace [ServerName] with the name of your web server)

The first time you launch the EMS Campus Planning Interface, it may take a few extra moments for the website to display.  If you encounter any issues, please contact support@emssoftware.com for assistance.
