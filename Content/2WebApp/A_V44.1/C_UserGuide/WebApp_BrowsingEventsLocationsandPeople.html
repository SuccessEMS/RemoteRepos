Browsing Events, Locations, and People

The BROWSE section of the main {{product_webapp}} menu enables you to explore Events, Locations, and People.  

Learn how to:

• Browse Events

• Browse Locations

• Browse People

Note: The previous version of this application, Virtual EMS ({{product_vems}}), had different menu options:

Menu in {{product_vems}} Menu in {{product_webapp}} How Is This Better?
My Requests Events Expands the view to all events, instead of only those you requested
Browse for Space Locations Expands the view to Facilities, Buildings, Views (custom collections of space), and Rooms
Browse Groups People Expands the view to Groups, individual Users, and custom user types.
