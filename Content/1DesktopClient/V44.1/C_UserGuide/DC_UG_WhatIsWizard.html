What is a Wizard?

Concept: Wizards are pop-up windows that enable end-users to make the same changes to more than one record at a time 

These powerful tools are available in several areas of EMS software applications, including EMS Desktop Client and EMS for Outlook. Additional wizards are provided for System Administrators to help them quickly configure elements on the back-end, such as currencies, rooms, resources, and building hours.
