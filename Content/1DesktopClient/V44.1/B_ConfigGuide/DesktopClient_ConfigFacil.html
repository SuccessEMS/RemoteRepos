Configuration > Facilities

Before you can use {{product}} to manage your organization’s events, you must configure core facilities that are specific to your organization. For example, before you can reserve a room, you must first configure the room. This menu is available on the {{product_dtc}} top menu bar under Configuration>Facilities.

* Floor Plan Diagrams and Floor Plan Indicators are optional features used when your organization has the optional add-on, EMS Floorplan.

Note: If your organization has purchased and installed {{product_campus}}, then you can synchronize data from your Student Information System (SIS) such as your campus buildings, rooms., and room types. As a result, before you carry out any configuration of your facilities data, see EMS Campus setup instructions.
