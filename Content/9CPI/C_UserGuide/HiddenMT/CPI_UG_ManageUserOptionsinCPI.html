Managing User Options in the EMS Campus Planning Interface

When your {{product}} administrator configures your user account, a password is set for your account. If you want, you can change your own password for the {{product_cpi}}. Also, your {{product}} Administrator might have configured custom help text that is displayed on pages in the {{product_cpi}}. You can turn off the display of this help text.
