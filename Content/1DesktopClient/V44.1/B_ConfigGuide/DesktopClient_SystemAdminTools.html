System Administration > Tools

You have several tools at your disposal in {{product}} to assist you in your administrative duties. These include a tool to update various group attributes such as pricing plans or account name, managing memorized reports, viewing logged-in users and if necessary, log these users off your {{product}} system, emailing logged-in users, deleting old data in your {{product}} database, and so on.
