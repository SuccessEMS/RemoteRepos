Removing Favorite Locations

When you no longer wish to see a location listed first in search results, you can remove it from your personalized list.

To remove a Favorite Location:

  1. Navigate to your Favorites list by clicking on the main menu in the upper left corner of {{product_mobile}} and selecting Favorite Locations.

  2. Click the Remove  icon to remove a Favorite from your list.
