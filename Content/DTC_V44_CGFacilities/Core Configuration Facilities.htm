Core Configuration – Facilities

Before you can use EMS to manage your organization’s events, you must configure core data items that are specific to your organization. For example, before you can reserve a room, you must first configure the room in EMS.

This chapter covers the following topics:

  • Configuring Floors
  • Configuring Features
  • Configuring Setup Types
  • Configuring Room Types
  • Configuring Buildings
  • Configuring Areas
  • Configuring Building Hours
  • Overview of Configuring Rooms
  • Importing Rooms
  • Configuring Room Pricing on the Edit Pricing window
  • Configuring Public Views
  • Configuring Floor Plans
  • Exporting Pricing Information for a Room
  • Configuring Rooms

Note: If your organization has purchased and installed EMS Academic Planning, then you can synchronize data from your Student Information System (SIS) such as your campus buildings, rooms, and room types. As a result, before you configure your facilities data, see EMS Campus setup instructions.
