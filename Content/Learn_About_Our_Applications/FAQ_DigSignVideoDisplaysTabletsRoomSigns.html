Digital Signage: Video Displays, Tablets, and Room Signs

Placed outside the entrance to meeting rooms, wall-mounted digital signs with EMS software allow employees to book themselves into reservable spaces. EMS provides the following, optional licenses to integrate with digital tablets and room signs.

{{product_emsk}}

Run on a touch-screen display unit, {{product_emsk}} is a powerful yet easy-to-use digital room sign and interactive tool for reviewing room availability, booking space, searching for a meeting location and more. Mount the unit outside a room and/or make an EMS Kiosk available in a lobby or entryway. Pair with the optional EMS Floor Plan module and give users an added measure of convenience as they see reserved/available space on a facility diagram, making it even easier to find  and hold meeting space. 
See Also: {{product_emsk}} Installation Overview.

{{product_roomsign}}

This web-based application turns your Apple iPad or Android tablet into a digital room sign, displaying real-time meeting information and at-a-glance room availability. It supports Apple, Android, and Microsoft tablets, and integrates with popular room sign products, such as Creston Connected Scheduling Application and Steelcase RoomWizard, a separate EMS module. See Also: {{product_roomsign}} Installation Overview.

Video Displays

The EMS Video Display Interface (VDI) is an add-on component that exports meeting and event information to a video display system such as Janus, Target Vision, and so on. 
See Also: VDI Installation Overview.
