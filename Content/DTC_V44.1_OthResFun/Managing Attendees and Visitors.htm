Managing Attendees and Visitors

Process Overview:

Visitor management is an optional feature that your administrator can enable on booking templates so that visitors can be added to your meetings in EMS Desktop Client, EMS Web App and VEMS. If the feature is enabled, you can:

  • View Visitors
  • Checking In Visitors
  • Import Lists of Visitors to a Meeting
  • Generate Visitor Reports
