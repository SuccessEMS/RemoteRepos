EMS offers a wide variety of authentication methods across its product offering. Since some of our solutions are web-based while others are Client-Server, options vary depending on the product. 

Supported Authentication Types

EMS products support different types of authentication. In addition to local machine authentication, EMS offers two optional features:

  • EMS Integrated Authentication: this component provides single-sign-on capability using Integrated Windows Authentication, your organization’s portal, or LDAP. See Also: Introduction to Integrated Windows Authentication.
  • EMS Integration to Exchange: this component enables your organization to leverage your existing Microsoft Exchange authentication policies. See Also: Introduction to Integration to Exchange.
