Reports and Queries

{{product}} contains six different categories of general reports to assist you in monitoring and maintaining your organization's reservation records and associated transactions. The application also contains a Query Builder function which you can use to define custom criteria for searching for information in your organization's {{product}} database.

This chapter covers the following topics:

  • "Generating an EMS Report".
  • "Creating Automated Reports"
  • "Working with Saved Reports".
  • "Report Descriptions and Options".
  • "Working with a Query".

Reports that are specific for the Billing function in {{product}} are detailed in "Billing."
