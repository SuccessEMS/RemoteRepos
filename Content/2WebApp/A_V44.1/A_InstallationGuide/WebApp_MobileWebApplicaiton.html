EMS Mobile Web Application

Concept: The {{product_webapp}} also runs on a mobile device such as a tablet or handheld. Due to the smaller screen size and resolution of tablets and smartphones, pages may display differently but most of the same functionality is still available.

{{product_mobile}}, by contrast, is an app designed specifically for smartphones. It offers functionality specifically for everyday users who need access to EMS while they're on the go. The {{product_mobile}} also runs on tablets that run iOS or Android, but the screen layout is optimized for smartphones and does not take advantage of larger displays.
