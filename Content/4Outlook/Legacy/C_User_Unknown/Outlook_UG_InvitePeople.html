Inviting People

{{product_emso}} enables you to invite individual attendees or groups of attendees, and carries over any invitees you entered in the To field (shown below) when you first began setting up the meeting in Microsoft® Outlook.

 

Your Administrator assigns the groups and attendees available to you in the dropdown field and Search option. Here you can look up, add, and remove groups from your meeting. Each member of a group will receive email notifications just as individual attendees do.

 

Tip: Your System Administrator may have set a different label for the Groups field, such as "Employees" or "Attendees."
