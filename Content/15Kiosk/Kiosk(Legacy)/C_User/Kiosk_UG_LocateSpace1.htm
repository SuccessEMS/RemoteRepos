Locating Space in EMS Kiosk (Legacy)

The Locate Space page in EMS Kiosk provides a broad perspective of all the available/unavailable space in the buildings in which today’s events are scheduled.

Locate Space page

After you open a floor map, you can click on any Available Room icon (which is typically a green icon) or an Unavailable Room icon (which is typically a red icon) on the map to view information about the room and the event.

Viewing information about an available room

To return to the Today’s Event page after viewing a floor map, click Home, which is displayed in the upper left corner of the Floor Map page.
