Using RSS Feeds in EMS Master Calendar

If your site administrator has configured RSS feeds, the RSS Feeds hyperlink will be available at the bottom of all pages in EMS Master Calendar.

Click on the link to see a list of all currently active RSS feeds. Click on the RSS button below a feed to open the feed. The first time you click the RSS Feeds link, you may be prompted to download an RSS feed reader.
