Viewing The Schedule

Page summary display

template('MindTouch/Controls/PageOverview');

Once you begin booking a meeting in Microsoft? Outlook, click the EMS for Outlook icon to see the schedule grid. This provides a calendar grid showing all bookings you are authorized to see.

 

Events on the grid are color-coded:

Setup and teardown times show as narrower bars bracketing the event. This is defined by your Administrator.

The view automatically shows attendees' free/busy schedules for the meeting date (attendees are listed in the left panel, and their schedule appears to the right in the grid). To hide availability schedules for attendees, de-select the Include Attendees In Schedule box in the lower right corner.

Filtering the View

You can quickly filter the schedule to only show events for a location, such as a building.

Tip: You can resize the left and right panels by clicking and dragging the borders between them, and your settings will be saved. Double-clicking the resize handle will reset the grid size to the default setting.

Concept: Events Can Be Named In Many Ways On The Schedule
Your Administrator controls how these are labeled in the grid; the example above shows meetings labeled by the person hosting the meeting (from the Group field).

Viewing Room Information

Click the Information icon next to a room to see more information about the room, such as its properties, geographic location, setup type, features, images (if available), and so on.
