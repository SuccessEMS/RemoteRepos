What is a Wizard?

 Concept: Wizards are pop-up windows that enable end-users to make the same changes to more than one record at a time 
These powerful tools are available in several areas of {{product}} software applications, including {{product_dtc}} and {{product_emso}}. Additional wizards are provided for System Administrators to help them quickly configure elements on the back-end, such as currencies, rooms, resources, and building hours.
