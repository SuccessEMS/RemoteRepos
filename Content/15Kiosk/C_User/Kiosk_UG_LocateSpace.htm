Locating a Space in EMS Kiosk App

The Locate Space page in EMS Kiosk provides a broad perspective of all the available/unavailable space in the buildings in which today’s events are scheduled.

Locate Space Page

  1. After you open a floor map, you can click on any Available Room icon (which is typically a green icon) or an Unavailable Room icon (which is typically a red icon) on the map to view information about the room and the event.
