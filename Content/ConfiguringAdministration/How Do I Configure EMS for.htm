Process Overview:

To configure functionality so that users can request space and those requests require approval, you will:

  • Identify space (rooms, buildings, facilities, services, etc.) you want to push through a Request>Approval process.
  • Determine who can make these requests, and who can approve them.
  • Define special process templates for this type of booking and associate them with managed space.
  • Assign these templates to users or types of users who should only be able to request managed space.
  • Guide approvers to use the EMS Desktop Client Dashboard to review and approve the requests.
