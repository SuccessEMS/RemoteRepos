Delete Meeting

Page summary display

template('MindTouch/Controls/PageOverview');
  1. Select the meeting to delete from the Web Scheduler calendar.
  2. Select Delete from the bottom left corner of the ‘Reservation Manager’ window.
  3. Select Confirm Deletion.

Note: Meetings deleted in Web Scheduler are automatically deleted in Outlook/ Exchange and other MeetingPlanner applications.
