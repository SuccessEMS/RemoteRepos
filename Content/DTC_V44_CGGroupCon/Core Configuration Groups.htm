Core Configuration - Groups and Contacts

A group is an entity for which reservations are made at your facilities. A group is one of the four core data items that you must configure in EMS so that your users can make reservations. A contact is a person who serves as the coordinator or focal point for a group.

  • Configuring Additional Information for a Group
  • Configuring Contacts
